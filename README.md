# Women Representation Analysis

## 📊 Objective
Analyze the growth of women's political representation across countries, focusing on identifying disparities between leading and lagging nations.

## 🧠 Approach
Instead of using absolute values, this analysis focuses on **growth rates** to better capture momentum and identify which countries are making the most progress over time.

## 📈 Visualization Strategy
- Two groups were analyzed: countries with the highest and lowest growth.
- A consistent axis scale was applied across both charts to ensure accurate visual comparison.
- Color intensity was used to reinforce ranking and highlight differences.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- There is a significant gap between leading and lagging countries in terms of growth.
- Countries such as **United Arab Emirates, Rwanda, and Bolivia** show the highest growth.
- This accelerated progress may be linked to **legislative reforms and policy changes**, suggesting that institutional actions play a key role in advancing women's political representation.
